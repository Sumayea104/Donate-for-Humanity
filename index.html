Why donate?

An earthquake has struck Turkey and many people are in need of assistance. Your donation can make a difference in providing the affected people with food, shelter, and medical care. By donating, you can help those who are most vulnerable and give them hope during these difficult times.

How to donate

Impact of your donation

Your donation will make a significant impact on the lives of those affected by the earthquake. It will help provide them with the essential resources they need to rebuild their lives and homes. Every donation, no matter how small, can help bring hope and support to those in need.
